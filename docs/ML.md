# Machine Learning

## Objective

Develop a machine learning system capable of accurately identifying wildlife from photographs.

The initial release focuses exclusively on bird species. The architecture is designed to expand to additional wildlife categories without requiring major changes.

---

## Version 1

* Bird identification
* Transfer learning
* Image classification
* Confidence scoring

---

## Future Versions

* Mammals
* Reptiles
* Amphibians
* Insects
* Marine life
* Plants
* Multi-species image detection
* Audio identification
* Video-based identification

---

## Candidate Models

* EfficientNet
* ConvNeXt
* Vision Transformer (ViT)

---

## Dataset Sources

* iNaturalist
* eBird
* GBIF
* Public research datasets

---

## Future Intelligence

Eventually the platform should learn from historical observations and environmental conditions to predict which species users are likely to encounter in a particular location.
